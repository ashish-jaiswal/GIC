About GIC

Location

  • All students come from nearby villages such as Shrikot, Morgi, Bamanti, and Chilote. These four villages had donated land for the school. Currently, our school has 28,620 square meters of land for school premises and other use.

  • Our school is situated at 17C, south-facing hilly and left side of the Barethi-Banchaura motor road. It is 70 kilometers away from the district and 18 kilometers from the Tehsil headquarters.

Infrastructure

The School building has been constructed in three different blocks. The building in the front was built in 1971 and has three classes 6th, 7th, and 8th. It also has a Mid-Day-Meal kitchen and an additional room for storage. The school also has offices for NCC and NSS. The central building has class 10 standard along with the sports room. The third block is newly constructed and has a principal office, an office room, a staff room, science laboratory, and library.
